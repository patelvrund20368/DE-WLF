About Us

  • ⇒ About WILDLIF RESCU
  •  At WILDLIF RESCU, we are passionate advocates for the well-being of animals and birds. Our mission is to provide a safe haven and care for these beautiful creatures in need, and to work tirelessly towards their protection, rehabilitation, and re-homing.

  •  Animal Help Now envisions a world in which humans:
  • Respect wildlife
  • Are familiar with the threats facing wildlife and act to minimize them
  • Are educated about wildlife emergencies and empowered to effectively help orphaned, injured and distressed wild animals
  • Are educated about living in harmony with wildlife and empowered to effectively and humanely resolve human/wildlife conflicts
  • Place a high value on the services provided by wildlife rehabilitators, humane wildlife conflict operators and other wildlife experts
  • ⇒ Our Mission
  •  Our core mission is simple yet profound: to rescue, rehabilitate, and advocate for the welfare of animals and birds. We believe that every life is precious, and it is our duty to ensure that animals and birds receive the love, care, and respect they deserve.
  • ⇒ What We Do
  • Rescue: We respond to calls for help, whether it's an injured bird or a neglected dog. Our dedicated team of professionals and volunteers is always ready to provide immediate assistance.
  • Rehabilitate: We provide expert medical care, nourishment, and a safe environment for animals and birds on their journey to recovery. Our rehabilitation programs focus on physical and emotional healing.
  • Advocate: We are committed to raising awareness about animal and bird welfare issues in our community and beyond. Through education and advocacy, we strive to create a world where animals and birds are treated with kindness and compassion.
  • ⇒ Our Values
  • Compassion: We approach every animal and bird with empathy and understanding, recognizing their unique needs and experiences.
  • Respect: We believe in treating all living beings with the utmost respect and dignity.
  • Dedication: Our team is fueled by an unwavering dedication to making a positive impact in the lives of animals and birds.
  • Transparency: We are committed to transparency in our operations and finances, ensuring that our supporters know their contributions are making a difference.
  • Thank you for visiting WILDLIF RESCU. Together, we can make a significant difference in the lives of animals and birds, one rescue, one rehabilitation, and one advocacy effort at a time.
